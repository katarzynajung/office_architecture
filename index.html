About us

B&FID is beautiful and functional interior design office. We are focused on designing comfortable, safe and useful indoor spaces from office area to ideally suited rooms for restful living.

As an employee-owned firm, our creative professionals are passionate, intense, and deeply client focused. We are inspired working alongside our clients to resolve complex issues and design highly comfortable environments where people live. Through our work we are trying to share our passion with our clients.
